# Warranty Terms

Acme Co's product warranty terms by category. Warranty coverage is separate from the refund policy and applies after the 30-day refund window has closed.

## Electronics

Electronics carry a 12-month limited warranty against manufacturing defects. The warranty starts on the order's `shipped_at` date, not the `paid_at` date. Coverage includes:

- Component failure under normal use.
- Battery capacity falling below 70% of rated capacity within the warranty period.
- Firmware-related issues that cannot be resolved by a factory reset.

Coverage does not include physical damage, water damage (unless the product is rated IPX7 or higher), or damage from unauthorized modifications. The warranty is voided if the device has been opened by anyone other than an authorized Acme repair partner.

## Apparel

Apparel carries a 90-day warranty against stitching defects, fabric defects, and dye-fastness issues. The warranty starts on the order's `shipped_at` date.

Apparel warranty claims do not cover wear-and-tear, fading from washing per the care label, or alterations performed by a third party. Customers who report a warranty issue within 90 days of `shipped_at` may choose between a free replacement (subject to stock) or a full refund credited to the original payment method.

## Home Goods

Home goods carry a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects. The warranty starts on the order's `shipped_at` date and covers structural failure of joints, glassware breakage during normal household use, and fabric defects on upholstered items.

Glassware warranty claims require a photo of the broken item; the customer is not required to return the broken piece (we ask that it be disposed of safely).

## Books

Books carry a 30-day warranty against printing defects (missing pages, blurred ink, binding failure). Beyond 30 days, book warranty claims are at the discretion of customer service.

## Food

Food products do not carry a warranty in the traditional sense. Quality issues with food products (off-flavor, expired-on-arrival, packaging compromise) are handled as refunds under the standard refund policy regardless of the 30-day window.

## What about expedited replacement?

A loyalty-tier customer at Gold or Platinum may request expedited warranty replacement; we ship the replacement before receiving the defective item back, provided the customer authorizes a hold on the original payment method. The hold is released when the defective item is received at our returns center.

Expedited replacement is not available for the electronics category due to fraud risk; electronics warranty claims always require the defective unit to be received first.
