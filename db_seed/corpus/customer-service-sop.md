# Customer Service Standard Operating Procedure

This SOP defines how Acme Co customer service agents handle inbound contacts, escalations, and special-case workflows.

## Contact Channels

Agents handle three channels: email (via the Zendesk-equivalent help desk), phone (queued through the unified contact center), and live chat (embedded in the customer portal). Email is the default channel for non-urgent contacts; phone is reserved for time-sensitive issues like delivery problems and active fraud reports.

Response-time targets:

- **Phone**: Answered within 90 seconds 95% of the time during business hours (Mon–Fri 8 AM – 8 PM Eastern).
- **Live chat**: First response within 60 seconds during business hours.
- **Email**: First response within 4 business hours; full resolution within 24 business hours for routine issues.

## Authentication

Before acting on a customer's behalf, agents must verify the customer's identity. For low-risk actions (looking up an order status, confirming a delivery address), name plus email match is sufficient. For account changes, refunds above $100, or any goodwill exception, the agent must also confirm the last four digits of the payment method on file or a recent order number.

Loyalty-tier Platinum members on the dedicated line are pre-authenticated through caller-ID match plus a one-time code sent to the email on file.

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to a supervisor when:

- The customer requests a refund above $500 outside the 30-day window.
- The customer has had three or more refunds in the trailing 6 months.
- The customer mentions legal action, regulatory complaints, or threatens public disclosure.
- The order shipped via expedited or international carrier and the customer is requesting full reimbursement including shipping.

Supervisor decisions are logged in the customer record. Agents should not promise resolution timelines on escalated cases; the supervisor commits the timeline after review.

## Tone and Phrasing

Agents address customers by first name only after the customer has used a first name themselves. Use "we" and "Acme Co" rather than "the company" or "they"; ownership of the problem is part of the brand.

Avoid the following phrases: "unfortunately," "as per policy," "I'm just the agent," and "the system won't let me." Each of these signals helplessness; substitute concrete next-step phrasing.

## Handling Negative Reviews

When a customer mentions an existing negative review (on social media, Trustpilot, etc.), agents should not request that the review be removed or modified in exchange for resolution. Acme Co's review-integrity policy explicitly forbids quid-pro-quo on reviews. Resolve the underlying issue first; the customer may choose to update the review on their own.

## After-Hours Coverage

The phone and live-chat channels close at 8 PM Eastern Monday through Friday and all day Saturday and Sunday. Email is monitored on weekends by a skeleton team with a 12-hour response-time target. Active fraud reports and delivery emergencies route to the on-call supervisor via a separate escalation queue.
