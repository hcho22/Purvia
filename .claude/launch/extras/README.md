# Extras

Chosen per brief §8 ("choose, don't sweep"): two extras that make the package feel most real, plus the required unexpected deliverable.

## The unexpected one: `datasheet.html` (PURVIA-1 Datasheet, Rev A)

A hardware-style component datasheet for a software kit, complete with operating conditions, absolute maximum ratings (the false-resolve ceiling as FRmax, the zero-leak bound as LEAKmax), measured characteristics with test conditions, verification methodology, ordering information, and a real errata sheet (PV-E1 through PV-E7) where the package's known weaknesses are filed with impact, workaround, and status, exactly like silicon errata.

Why this is the one: it is the artifact only this product could ship. Every AI startup has a launch thread and a landing page; none of them ships errata. It is the founder's ten years of hardware validation made literal, and every number in it traces to `../evidence/claims.md`. Rendered and reviewed: `datasheet-screenshot.png`. Self-contained HTML, opens locally, print-friendly.

## Profile assets: `profile-assets/`

Avatar (800x800), X banner (1500x500), LinkedIn banner (1584x396), all rendered from `banners.html` with the brand tokens and reviewed; per-platform bios written to each platform's character limit in `bios.md`. No accounts were created; nothing was uploaded.

## Deliberately not built

Ad creatives (no channel has earned spend before kill-criterion data exists), pitch deck (no fundraise), email sequence (no list; sending anything violates the publish guardrail). One polished, verified artifact beats three rushed ones.
