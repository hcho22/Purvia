The business, in five minutes

The product (exists, MIT, public at github.com/hcho22/Purvia): a permissions-aware RAG kit where the two claims that matter are checked by evals in CI rather than asserted. Chunk-level ACLs are enforced inside the retrieval SQL predicate under the viewer's JWT; zero-leak and API-attack evals fail their required CI check on any breach; a deterministic escalation gate keeps the support bot under a buyer-set 5% false-resolve ceiling. Raw OpenAI SDK + Pydantic; no orchestration framework in the product.

The brand thesis (won a 5-pitch, 3-judge tournament, 61.7/65): the receipts are the product. The centerpiece is a confession with a paper trail: the kit's lexical retrieval leg shipped dead, its own nightly eval published that fact in 33 straight snapshots over seven weeks, nobody read them, and when a competitor comparison finally forced a look, the fix landed the same day and the same public series verified it (0.140 to 0.917 keyword recall@5). Every claim in the package terminates in a file path or a fetched URL, catalogued in a 90-row claims ledger with 16 anti-claims that ban specific overstatements.

The buyer: production-minded engineers adding RAG to real multi-user products, burned by framework churn, whose documents must not leak across users. The bet, falsifiable: receipts change adoption behavior; if eval receipts do not out-convert feature lists for this ICP, the thesis is wrong. Kill criteria with numbers and clocks are in the THESIS.

The honest state: launch is currently gated. Eight preconditions must clear first, including a permissions-scale benchmark that has published 0.000 in every cell since 06-19 (disclosed everywhere, including a red badge on the landing page), a README that still contradicts it, and an unverified founder bio. The red team rated launching over these a kill; the content calendar is hard-gated on them. Zero users, zero revenue; the package says so in its own copy.

  • The buyer's overall verdict in RED_TEAM.md, which concludes the honest disclosures assemble into a case against adoption. It is quoted in full, undeleted, because if the receipts thesis cannot survive its own red team being published, it is not a receipts thesis. Whether that verdict or the thesis wins is exactly what the THESIS kill criteria measure after launch.
