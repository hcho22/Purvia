# Brand tournament: rubric and scorecards

Five independent agents pitch a complete positioning + name + pricing-framing thesis without seeing each other's work.
Three judges score every pitch against the rubric below, independently, then scores are averaged.
The rubric is published here before any pitch is scored (brief §5).
Scorecards for all pitches, including the losers and why they lost, are appended below after judging.

## Constraints every pitch inherits (non-negotiable)

- Ground truth rule: every claim must be cashable against `launch/evidence/claims.md`; contradicting the anti-claims (section J) disqualifies the pitch.
- The known weaknesses in `launch/THESIS.md` must be worked with, not around.
- Voice rules of the brief §4 apply to all pitch copy.
- Naming: "Purvia" is the committed incumbent (ADR-0011, merged 2026-07-15). A pitch may challenge it only with evidence on searchability, collision, or fit; a successful challenge must beat the switching cost of re-renaming days after a merged rebrand.
- Commercial shape: open core, pro tier $149-$249, challengeable only with evidence (per brief §0).

## Rubric (score each axis 1-5; weights in parentheses)

1. **Evidence strength (x3).** Every load-bearing sentence in the pitch traces to a claims.md ID. Deductions for any claim that would need a new unverified fact.
2. **Defensibility against a skeptical buyer (x3).** Would the pitch survive the staff engineer who clicks the CI badge and reads the eval JSON? Deductions for anything that reads as spin when placed next to the repo.
3. **Fit to the actual repo (x2).** The pitch sells what the artifact is (a kit + eval harness with disclosed weaknesses), not an aspirational platform.
4. **Distinctiveness (x2).** Is it distinguishable from every other 2026 AI-tool launch? Deductions for interchangeable positioning ("secure RAG made easy") that any competitor could paste over their logo.
5. **Non-interference with the job search (x2).** A hiring manager reading this should think "rigorous engineer," never "grifter." Deductions for hype residue, category-king cosplay, or anything that would embarrass in an interview readout.
6. **Falsifiability (x1).** The pitch's central promise can be checked by a stranger in under ten minutes with the repo open.

Maximum weighted score: 65.

## Agent budget

5 pitchers + 3 judges + 1 synthesis pass = 9 agents, chosen to keep the tournament inside a single workflow and leave context budget for Phases 3-6 (brief §5 warns against orchestration that burns the window).
