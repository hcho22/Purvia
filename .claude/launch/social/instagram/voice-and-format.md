# Instagram: voice and format

**Logic of the platform for this launch:** Instagram is the explainer-card channel. The audience is engineers who save single-idea visual explainers. Nothing converts here directly; the job is recognition (the "receipts" visual system) and saves. The card carries the claim; the caption carries the voice; the source path rides on the card itself so a screenshot keeps its citation.

Format rules:
- One receipt per card. The card must be legible at feed size: headline 60px+, body 30px+, mono for every number.
- 1080x1350 (4:5 portrait). Brand tokens only; the green accent means "verified" and is never decorative; red appears only on disclosed failures.
- Captions are 3-6 sentences of founder voice, first person, no emoji, no exclamation marks, no em dashes. The caption never repeats the card text verbatim; it adds the story or the caveat.
- Alt text is mandatory per post and describes the data, not just the design.
- Hashtags: 4-6, first comment, lowercase, no invented tags.
- Every card footer carries the repo URL; "link in bio" appears only when the caption directs to a specific doc.

Regeneration: see `feed-concept.md` (cards.html + headless Chromium; all cards re-rendered and re-viewed after any copy change).
