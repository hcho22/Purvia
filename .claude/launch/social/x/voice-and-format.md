# X: voice and format

**Logic of the platform for this launch:** X is where the skeptical engineer lives. Every post leads with the receipt (a number plus its path), never the pitch. The reply to "source?" is always a repo path that resolves; if a post cannot survive that reply, it does not ship.

Format rules:
- One receipt per post. Number + unit + caveat + path.
- Length rule, amended after the red team measured the drafts (BR-3): posts use the long-post format where needed, but the receipt (the number and its path) must land inside the first 280 characters, above the fold. Posts that can fit 280 should.
- No rhetorical-question openers, including in thread tweets (the linter cannot hear these; check by hand).
- No hashtags on X (they read as growth-hacking to this audience). No emoji in body copy. No exclamation marks. No em dashes.
- First person singular. "I shipped", "I found", "I have not proven".
- Threads: one idea per tweet; the last tweet is always the honest-limits tweet.
- Weakness disclosure is content, not damage control: X-09 is the build-in-public post about the currently-broken benchmark, required by the launch plan.
- Strip the bracketed claim IDs before posting; they are audit trail, not copy.

Cadence: launch thread on day 1, then one standalone post per weekday. Never two receipts posts within 4 hours; replies get priority over new posts.
