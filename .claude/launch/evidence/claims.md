## A. The dead-leg arc (the centerpiece receipt)

The honest arc, in one paragraph, which all copy must follow:
the lexical leg of hybrid retrieval shipped dead and the nightly harness put that on the public record from its first snapshot (2026-05-19, keyword recall@5 0.110) through 33 consecutive snapshots over 7 weeks.
The one automated alarm, a two-sided baseline check, was stuck in a permanent false-alarm state that flagged even improvements, so nobody read it.
A 2026-07-10 comparison against another project prompted the first real read; the fix was scoped that day from the harness's own published numbers and landed by 07-13, and the harness verified the fix in the same public series.
The harness's contribution was a timestamped public record that made the failure undeniable and the fix measurable, plus the US-118 lesson that an alarm that always fires is no alarm.
