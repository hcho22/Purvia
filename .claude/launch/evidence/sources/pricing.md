## Pattern summary (across the sources above)

What paid tiers gate, in descending frequency across these 11 sources:
updates (lifetime or 1 year), support (Discord/email/priority), advanced or "full" feature set vs a basic free core, seats/projects (license scope), and pre-built modules/templates.
One-time lifetime pricing dominates code-you-own products (boilerplates, component kits: ShipFast, Pegasus, MakerKit, Supastarter, Tailwind Plus, Shadcnblocks, StartKit.AI); subscriptions dominate when the vendor runs infrastructure or ships continuous server-side value (Sidekiq, Tiptap, Excalidraw+).
$149-$249 one-time sits squarely inside the observed entry-tier band ($149 Shadcnblocks Pro, $149/$249 StartKit.AI, $199/$249 ShipFast) and below the $299-$449 mid band (Tailwind Plus, MakerKit, Supastarter, Pegasus).
